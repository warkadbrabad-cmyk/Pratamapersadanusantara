About Us

Heritage of Indonesian
Craftsmanship

PT Pratama Persada Nusantara is a leading rattan furniture manufacturer and exporter based in Cirebon, West Java — Indonesia's premier hub for rattan craftsmanship. We bridge the timeless artistry of Indonesian master weavers with the sophisticated demands of the global market.

Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and ethical production has earned us the trust of buyers across Europe, North America, Australia, and the Middle East. Every piece we create tells a story of tradition, precision, and natural beauty.
